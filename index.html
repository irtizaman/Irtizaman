About Me



I’m a student fueled by a passion for exploring the intersection of technology, art, and media. I love to create—whether it’s content, tools, games, or whatever sparks my curiosity. I believe creativity knows no boundaries, so I dive into anything I can get my hands on.

From Photoshop and Unity to SolidWorks, music production, and beyond, I enjoy working with a wide range of tools to bring my ideas to life. My projects often combine entertainment with interactive elements, using humor and storytelling to engage users in unique ways.

No idea is too random—I’m always juggling a few creative projects at once, thanks to my ADHD-fueled curiosity. As a broke uni student, I’ve learned to be resourceful, using creativity to solve problems and stretch my budget. Whether it’s tech experiments, digital experiences, or collaborative work, I aim to make things that inspire, entertain, and leave a lasting impact.
